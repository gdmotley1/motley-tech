▸ ten most common · no login · no email required to read
> q_01

Is the free build really free?

Yes, completely. I build the full site, you try it, and if you don't want to keep it we part ways with no hard feelings and no bill. You only pay if you love it — either a monthly hosting subscription, or a one-time fee to own the code outright.

> q_02

What's the catch?

I'm early in the business and building a portfolio. The first few clients get a better deal than later ones ever will — I'd ask to feature the finished site as a case study, and in return you get a permanent founder's rate if you stick around on the subscription.

> q_03

How long until my site is live?

Typically about 14 days from our first call. Simpler sites (single service, straightforward menu or product list) can be faster. Sites with lots of custom features, heavy content, or complex integrations may take longer — I'll tell you up front.

> q_04

What happens if I stop paying for hosting later?

Zero lock-in. I give you 60 days notice either way. Your options: switch to a buyout (one-time fee, code is yours, take it anywhere), migrate to another host with my help, or stay on your founder's rate. No surprise charges, no ransom.

> q_05

Can I update the site myself?

Yes, if you want. I can set up a simple content editor so you can change text, swap photos, update hours and prices from your phone. Most clients just text me updates though — same-day turnaround is part of the subscription.

> q_06 · ai search

Will Google and AI find me?

Every site I build is optimized for both traditional search (Google, Bing) and AI answer engines (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Claude). That means proper meta tags, Schema.org structured data, fast load times, mobile-first design, and clear content that AI tools can confidently cite when someone asks them for a recommendation.

> q_07

How does this compare to Squarespace / Wix / WordPress?

Template builders work fine if you want a generic $20-a-month site that looks like every other business in town. I build something specifically for your business — custom design, faster, cleaner, more memorable. If you ever want to move to Squarespace later, the site is standard HTML so your content is fully portable.

> q_08

What if I already have a website?

Even better. I'll audit your existing site free — tell you what's working and what's costing you customers. If a rebuild makes sense, we go from there. The free build still applies, and you only pay if you prefer the new one.

> q_09

Can I keep my current domain name?

Yes. Whatever domain you own today stays yours — I just point it at the new site. If you don't have one yet, I'll help you pick and register a domain that actually matches your business.

> q_10

Do you work with businesses outside your area?

Yes. I start local because face-to-face meetings are the fastest way to learn a business, but the build process itself works anywhere. If you're a small business anywhere in the US, reach out.
