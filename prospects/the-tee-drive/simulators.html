The tracking

What gets measured gets better.

The same tracking standard used on tour. Every swing is logged in real time, so you leave knowing exactly what to work on. The eight numbers that matter most:

Ball speed
How fast the ball leaves the face. The single biggest predictor of distance.
168 MPH · TOUR AVG
Club speed
Club head velocity at impact. The input that drives every other number.
113 MPH · TOUR AVG
Smash factor
Ball speed divided by club speed. How cleanly you found the center.
1.50 · ELITE
Launch angle
Degrees of climb off the face. The difference between carry and roll.
10-14° · OPTIMAL DRIVER
Spin rate
RPM on the ball. Too much balloons the flight, too little won't hold a green.
2,500-2,800 · DRIVER
Spin axis
Tilt of the spin. Positive fades, negative draws. Now you know which is which.
±5° · STRAIGHT
Club path
In-to-out or out-to-in through impact. The root cause of slices and hooks.
0° · NEUTRAL
Face angle
Where the face actually pointed at impact, relative to your target line.
±1° · TOUR LEVEL

Plus carry, total, apex, descent angle, attack angle, dynamic loft, spin loft, swing direction, low point, and more. Toggle on what you want. Ignore the rest.
