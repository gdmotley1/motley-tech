[ // 05 ] QUESTIONS

Asked + answered.

Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes, monthly. Cancel through your account or in person. Founder spots don't refund the prorated month, but you keep access until the period ends.
What counts as a visit? +
Any 1-hour bay block. Golf, darts, doesn't matter. Multiple back-to-back hours count as one booking, not multiple visits.
Can I share my membership? +
Regular is single-person. Founder allows two named players on one account, both get full member benefits. Add additional named players for $40/mo each.
Do guest passes roll over? +
On Regular, yes, up to 6 banked. After 6, new passes don't accrue until you spend down. Founder is unlimited so this doesn't apply.
How does league night work? +
Wednesday nights, 8-week seasons, three formats: stroke, scramble, closest-to-pin series. $10 buy-in for non-members, free for Founders. Prizes vary by season.
What if I sign up and never come in? +
You're paying us to hold a Founder spot you're not using. That's allowed, and frankly appreciated. But we'd rather see you on a Tuesday.
